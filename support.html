Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post an ad?
Click on the "Post Ad" button on the top right corner. Fill in the details of your item, upload photos, and submit. Your ad will be live after a quick review.
Is it free to sell?
Yes! Basic ads are completely free. We also offer premium features to boost your ad visibility for a small fee.
How do I edit or delete my ad?
Go to your dashboard, find the ad you want to manage, and click Edit or Delete. Changes are reflected immediately.
How do I contact a seller?
Click "Chat" or "Call" on the ad details page to connect directly with the seller through our secure messaging system.
Is my personal data safe?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.
How do I report a suspicious ad?
Click the "Report" button on the ad details page. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours and takes appropriate action.
